Inserted text

The <ins> tag indicates content that has been added after the original publication.

Today I went to the zoo. I also went to Sea World.


Deleted text

The <del> tag indicates content that has been deleted from the original publication.

I am studying Computer Science. I expect to graduate in 2014.
